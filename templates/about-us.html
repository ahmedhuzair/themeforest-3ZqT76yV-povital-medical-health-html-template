Who We Are ?

"We are your trusted partner in health, providing precise diagnostics with care and expertise."

Established in 2021, CarePlus Diagnostic Services LLC calls Highland, Illinois, its home. However, our vision extends far beyond, as we aim to bring our compassionate and efficient ultrasound diagnostic services to multiple states across the U.S. We have made it our mission to collaborate with long-term care facilities, short-term rehabilitation centers, assisted/independent living facilities, group homes, and home healthcare agencies to improve the quality of healthcare provided to individuals in need.

At the core of our company's inception is a strong belief in the power of humanity to alleviate the difficulties faced by others. We began by asking ourselves a fundamental question: What level of care would we desire for our own loved ones in times of adversity? This question inspired our commitment to providing exceptional care to all those we serve.

Our commitment is reinforced by the advancements in technology that have made ultrasound machines portable and enabled us to deliver rapid, accurate results. We understand that when it comes to health, time is of the essence. As a result, we have embraced the latest technology to expedite the diagnostic process, ensuring that the care we offer to our clients is the same care we would want for our own family members in their time of need.
